Biryani Recipe

Hyderabadi biryani, also known as Hyderabadi dum biryani, is a style of biryani from Hyderabad, India made with basmati rice and meat (mostly chicken). Originating in the kitchens of the Nizam of Hyderabad, it combines elements of Hyderabadi and Mughlai cuisines. Hyderabad biryani is a key dish in Hyderabadi cuisine and it is so famous that the dish is considered synonymous with the city of Hyderabad.

    Ingredients
  • half kilogram chicken
  • plain yogurt
  • garam masala or biryani masala
  • salt
  • ground turmeric
  • red chili powder
  • lemon juice
    Steps
  1. Mix all the aforementioned ingredients together
  2. Mix everything well and marinate the chicken in a large bowl. Cover and set aside for an hour.
  3. Wash 2 cups of basmati rice. Drain and soak the rice in a large bowl.
    Cooking the chicken
  1. Heat ghee and add the following spices. Bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, namon, star anise, caraway seeds, mace
  2. Add thinly sliced onions and fry them until golden brown
  3. Add the marinated chicken and fry them until the skin is golden brown
  4. cover the chicken and wait until the chicken is cooked and tender
    Making the Biryani
  1. Mix everything well and layer into a level layer
  2. layer the drained rice over the chicken and add water
  3. cook until fully cooked
  4. Serve
